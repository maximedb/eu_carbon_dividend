European Carbon Dividend

Tax carbon usage and redistribute the money raised as a basic income to the european citizens.

How does it work ?

1

Fuels are taxed at their point of origin into the economy, based on their carbon content.

2

The tax starts at 10€ per ton of carbon, and increases by 10€ every year.

3

Money raised through the tax is redistributed in equal amount to the European people.

4

A border tax adjustment is levied on imports from nations that lack their own equivalent fee on carbon.
